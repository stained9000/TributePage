Kurt Cobain

A musician that defined a generation.

Here's a timeline on Kurt Cobain's life:

  • 1967 - Born at Grays Harbor Hospital in Aberdeen, Washington, the son of waitress Wendy Elizabeth and automotive mechanic Donald Leland Cobain.
  • 1985 - Formed the band Fecal Matter.
  • 1987 - Formed the band Nirvana with Krist Novoselic.
  • 1988 - Recruited drummer Chad Channing to Nirvana.
  • 1989 - Nirvana recorded the album Bleach and was released on Sub Pop Records.
  • 1990 - Nirvana lets go Chad Channing and recruits Dave Grohl as drummer for the band.
  • 1991 - Nirvana records debut album Nevermind which popularized the subgenre of alternative rock called grunge.
  • 1992 - Married to Courtney Love.
  • 1992 - His daughter Frances Bean Cobain was born.
  • 1994 - Kurt commits suicide in his house.
